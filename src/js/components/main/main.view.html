Catchy Coffee Tagline

At Séamus Roasters, we like good coffee. We also dislike bad coffee. When we encounter mediocre coffee, we treat it the same way we'd treat bad coffee. That's why we started roasting: because now we can make all the good coffee we want, and never have to think about that bad, or mediocre, or half-assed, or only decent, or "meh" coffee.
